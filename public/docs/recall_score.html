Algorithm

Recall Score

Description

Recall, or Sensitivity (True Positive Rate), measures the proportion of actual positives that were identified correctly. It answers the question: 'Of all the people who truly have the disease, how many did we diagnose?' High recall relates to low false negative rate.

$$ \text{Recall} = \frac{TP}{TP + FN} $$

Use Cases

Medical Diagnosis (Minimize False Negatives)
